Day 1 - CRM Basics
What is CRM?

CRM stands for - Customer Relationship Management.

It helps companies manage customers, sales, communication, and business workflow.

Why Companies Use Salesforce

* Manage customer data
* Track sales opportunities
* Improve customer service
* Automate business processes
* Access data from anywhere



Salesforce Concepts

1.Account

Represents a company or organization.

2.Contact

Represents a person related to the account.

3.Opportunity

A possible business deal or process.


 Business Flow

Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer
